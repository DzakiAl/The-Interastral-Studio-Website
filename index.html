About Us

The Interastral Studio is an independent game studio founded with a passion for storytelling and creativity. We focus on developing story-driven games that aim to connect with players on a deeper level through immersive narratives and engaging experiences.

Our goal is to grow into a successful studio that values creativity, quality, and community feedback. While we're just getting started, we're committed to building games that leave a lasting impact.

This is where our journey begin and we're excited for what lies ahead.

Our Team

Dzaki Al

Founder and CEO

Dzaki Al is a multidisciplinary game developer with a strong focus on programming, 3D modeling, and narrative design. Driven by a lifelong passion for video games, he began his journey with a simple goal, to create his own unique experiences for others to enjoy.

With expertise in tools like Unity and Blender, Dzaki combines technical skill with creative storytelling to develop engaging, story-driven games. Whether building immersive environments or crafting compelling plots, he brings both precision and imagination to every project.
