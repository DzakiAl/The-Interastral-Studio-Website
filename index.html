About Us

The Interastral Studio is an independent game studio founded with a passion for storytelling and creativity. We focus on developing story-driven games that aim to connect with players on a deeper level through immersive narratives and engaging experiences.

Our goal is to grow into a successful studio that values creativity, quality, and community feedback. While we're just getting started, we're committed to building games that leave a lasting impact.

This is where our journey begin and we're excited for what lies ahead.
